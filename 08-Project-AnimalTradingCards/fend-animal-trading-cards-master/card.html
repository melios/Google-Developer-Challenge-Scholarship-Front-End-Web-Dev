Gremlins

Gremlins are the fictional and unknown reptile-like creatures, who are the primary antagonists of the entire Gremlins franchise.

  • Height: Approx. 3 feet
  • First Appearance: Gremlins
  • Last Appearance: Gremlins 2: The New Batch
  • Abilities: Metamorphosis, Spawning new gremlins, Evolution

In both movies, the gremlins have a leader that is smarter than all others. Stripe is the leader in the first movie and Mohawk is the leader in the second movie.
